Blue Light Mitigation

Artificial blue light from screens suppresses melatonin production, disrupting sleep and increasing oxidative stress in the eyes. Mitigation strategies include using blue-blocking glasses after sunset and installing software like f.lux to shift screen color temperature.

Protecting your circadian rhythm from digital interference is critical for maintaining deep sleep quality and next-day cognitive performance.
