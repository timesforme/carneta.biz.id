The Science of Sun Exposure

Natural sunlight exposure is essential for Vitamin D synthesis and serotonin production. UVB rays stimulate the production of Vitamin D3, while infrared light helps repair cells and reduce inflammation.

Safe, gradual exposure to the sun is one of the most fundamental yet overlooked health interventions for long-term well-being.
