Nutrigenomics

Genetic-Based Nutrition Plans

The era of the "one size fits all" diet is over. Nutrigenomics is the study of how individual genetic variation affects response to nutrients. By analyzing your DNA, you can uncover how your body processes fats, carbohydrates, and specific micronutrients.

Precision Fueling

Some individuals possess genetic variants like the FTO gene that make them more prone to weight gain from saturated fats, while others may have variations in the MTHFR gene affecting how they process B-vitamins. Understanding these genetic predispositions allows for the creation of a precision nutrition plan that optimizes energy and prevents chronic inflammation.

By eating in alignment with your evolutionary blueprint, you can unlock energy levels you never thought possible while dramatically reducing the risk of lifestyle diseases.
