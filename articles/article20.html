Exogenous Ketones

Exogenous ketones are supplements that provide the body with Beta-Hydroxybutyrate (BHB), bypassing the need for a strict ketogenic diet to enter a state of ketosis. They are used for instant cognitive clarity and sustained physical endurance.

While not a replacement for healthy habits, they provide an efficient alternative fuel source for the brain during intense mental tasks.
