The Role of Melatonin

Melatonin is often marketed as a sleep aid, but it is also one of the body's most potent antioxidants, particularly within the mitochondria. It helps repair DNA and clear neural waste during sleep.

Instead of relying on supplements, optimizing natural melatonin through light timing and darkness ensures the body maintains its natural repair mechanisms.
