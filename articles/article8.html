Cellular Energy

Optimizing Mitochondria

Mitochondria are the powerhouses of our cells, responsible for producing ATP (Adenosine Triphosphate). Mitochondrial dysfunction is a hallmark of aging and chronic fatigue.

Boosting ATP Production

Supplements like CoQ10, PQQ, and L-Carnitine are often used to support mitochondrial health, but lifestyle factors like zone 2 cardio and red light therapy are equally powerful. By exposing your cells to specific wavelengths of light, you can stimulate cytochrome c oxidase, enhancing the electron transport chain.

Efficient energy production is the foundation of high-performance living. When your mitochondria are thriving, your brain and body feel a level of energy that caffeine cannot replicate.
