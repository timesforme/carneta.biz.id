HIIT & Metabolic Health

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is one of the most efficient ways to improve cardiovascular health and mitochondrial density. Short bursts of maximal effort followed by brief periods of rest trigger EPOC (Excess Post-exercise Oxygen Consumption), keeping your metabolism elevated for hours after the workout.

Consistent HIIT training improves insulin sensitivity and stimulates the production of BDNF, a protein that supports brain health and new neuron growth.
