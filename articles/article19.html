Grounding & Inflammation

Grounding, or earthing, involves direct physical contact with the Earth's surface. This allows the body to absorb free electrons, which act as natural antioxidants to neutralize positive charge and inflammation.

Regular grounding has been associated with improved sleep, reduced muscle soreness, and balanced cortisol levels, making it a simple yet effective wellness practice.
