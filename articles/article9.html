Nootropics & Cognitive Peak

Nootropics, or "smart drugs," are substances that enhance cognitive function, specifically executive functions, memory, creativity, or motivation, in healthy individuals.

The Neuro-Enhancement Frontier

Natural nootropics like Lion's Mane mushroom and Bacopa Monnieri support nerve growth factor (NGF) and memory retention. Synthetic compounds are being studied for their ability to enhance focus during periods of sleep deprivation. However, at Carneta, we believe that nootropics work best when layered upon a foundation of sleep and nutrition.

Developing a personalized "stack" can provide a competitive edge in high-stakes environments, allowing for sustained focus without the crash common with stimulants.
